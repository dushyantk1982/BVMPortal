About BVM Group

Welcome to the site of the BVM Group - a premier organization in Madhya Pradesh administering Eleven Colleges run by Bhartiya Vidya Mandir Shiksha Samiti, an eminent educational society had been established in 1969 registered under M.P. Societies registration Act.1869(106-1960).

Since inception in 1996, the Bhartiya Vidya Mandir Institutions have come to be known as centres of excellence in the field of Management , Computers & Teaching education promoting national integration and a sense of "Indianness" among the Students while ensuring their total personality development, academic and professional excellence. Discover how we are laying the foundation of India's tomorrow.

Our Vision & Mission.

To contribute to the creation and advancement of knowledge,to educate and train responsible and enlightened citizens and qualified specialists. To develop ourselves into a premier Institute of Management and Computer Education with focus on Quality, Excellence and Innovation.
